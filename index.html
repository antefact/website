HOW DOES IT WORK?

1. Dive and prepare

We dive in your company/studio to learn about your process and needs. We start by creating ad-hoc hands-on workshops to start working together and learn about the focus of the project.

2. Iterations (i * n)

We build-test-learn to get to the level of quality that you need. We can start from simple paper and cardboard for quick experiences and get all the way to standalone prototypes with custom electronics and software to test with people.

3. Document and Share

We document the process and create shareable learnings for you. From pictures to short videos, to prototyping kits we close the loop of the project leaving you the tools to convince others or to continue prototype and test by yourself.
